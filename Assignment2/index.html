Serious Crime In New York City

The purpose of the Visualisation is to gain insights about the crime zones of Serious Criminal Offences and the trend of crime rate between January 2006 to December 2018 in New York City. 5 Serious Criminal Offences will be discussed in the visualisation including:

Murder • Rape • Theft-fraud • Kidnapping • Arson

New York City (NYC) is the most populous city in the United States. With an estimated 2019 population of 8,336,817 distributed over about 302.6 square miles (784 km2), New York City is also the most densely populated major city in the United States. Located at the southern tip of the U.S. state of New York, the city is the center of the New York metropolitan area, the largest metropolitan area in the world by urban landmass. With almost 20 million people in its metropolitan statistical area and approximately 23 million in its combined statistical area, it is one of the world's most populous megacities.
